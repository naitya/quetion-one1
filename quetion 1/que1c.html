A doctor is a medical professional who has completed the necessary education and training to diagnose.

Medicine refers to the field of knowledge, practice, and science focused on maintaining, diagnosing, treating, and preventing illnesses and injuries in humans and animals. It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including biology, chemistry, anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, and psychology, among others. Medical professionals, such as doctors, nurses, and specialists, apply their expertise to understand the intricacies of the human body's functions, identify health issues through clinical examinations and diagnostic tests, and prescribe appropriate treatments, which can include medications, surgeries, therapies, and lifestyle interventions. Medicine is not only concerned with curing diseases but also emphasizes preventive measures to promote overall well-being. The field has advanced significantly over the years, incorporating cutting-edge technologies and research to enhance healthcare practices and contribute to the betterment of individuals and society as a whole.
